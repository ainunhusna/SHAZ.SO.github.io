ABOUT US

Please keep the cake to chiller within 30 minutes to avoid melting and maintain the quality of the cake. All cakes must be kept refrigerated (2°c – 6°c) at all times before serving.

All cakes are made using fresh ingredients, SHAZ.SO strongly recommend to consume the cake within the same day of purchase. If there’s any leftovers, please keep the cake fully sealed in an air-tight plastic container in the fridge to prevent dehydration/contamination and consume as soon as possible.
